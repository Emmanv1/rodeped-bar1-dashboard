Key observations & alerts — Q1 2025
Infrastructure gap — classrooms constructed
Only 36 of 60 targeted classrooms were constructed in Q1 (60% accomplishment). Surigao del Sur contributed 30, Butuan City 6. Most SDOs reported zero completions.
Equipment distribution severely behind target
Sci/Math equipment: only 1,156 of 210,286 targeted units distributed (0.5%). TVL: 71 of 2,887. ICT: 237 of 712. Many SDOs reported no delivery in Q1.
Training accomplishment at 30% of annual target
2,674 personnel trained vs target of 8,828 for Q1. SDO Surigao del Norte led with 480 teachers trained. Surigao del Sur reported no training data.
LAC sessions — schools below target
968 schools reported conducting LAC vs target of 1,650–1,651. Several SDOs (Agusan del Sur, Surigao del Norte, Surigao del Sur) did not report school-level data.
NAT data limited — only 2 SDOs with complete results
Siargao and Surigao del Sur provided NAT scores. Elem: regional actual 30.2% vs 46% target. JHS: 24.6% vs 43.6%. SHS: 34.7% vs 45.3%. Most SDOs reported NDA or no data yet.
BERF proposals — 19 approved regionally
Tandag City led with 6 approvals, followed by Bislig City and Dinagat Islands with 4 each. 5 SDOs had no BERF proposals approved in Q1.
Policies reviewed — on target
RO reviewed 2 policies in Q1 (HRDD & PPRD), matching the Q1 target of 2. Education researches completed and proposed policies to be determined in Q4.
